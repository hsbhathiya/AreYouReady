CV Clinic

8 oct 2013
Anushka Abeyrathna & Sameera Gunarathne Curriculum Vitae Workshop


Introduction


Curriculum Vitae is the first image of the job seeker that impresses the employer. This session was initiated for the first time at an “Are You Ready” program on behalf of the undergraduates of the University to offer the proficiency of preparing the most important piece, the Curriculum Vitae before entrance to the corporate world. On the outset of the session a guest speaker will be giving the proper guidance on how to prepare the Curriculum Vitae and its significance. Then the past Rotaractors of the University who are fully engaged professionals in the industry will give individual attention for the undergraduates with their prepared CVs pointing out revisions.


Contents


  • Guest speech on how to prepare a proper CV and its significance
  • Interactive session that examine the undergraduates’ CVs by the past Rotarians and Rotaractors
